Education Background

SK Mutiara Perdana

Mutiara Perdana National School or abbreviated as SK Mutiara Perdana , is a national school located on Jalan Dato Ismail Hashim. In 2009, Sekolah Kebangsaan Mutiara Perdana had 557 male students and 535 female students, making a total of 1092 students. It had 58 teachers.

SMK Telok Kumbar

Telok Kumbar National Secondary School or its short name SMK Telok Kumbar , is a national secondary school located on Jalan Telok Kumbar. In 2023, Telok Kumbar National High School had 408 male students and 355 female students, making a total of 762 students. It had 54 teachers.
