My Skills

I have a passion for creating content that blends creativity, culture, and authenticity. One of my favorite ways to express this is through cooking on camera, where I not only prepare meals but also enjoy presenting the process as a form of storytelling.

Behind the lens, I love capturing spontaneous moments and bringing them to life using Adobe Lightroom, especially by experimenting with filters and lens distortion to add depth and emotion to my photos.

My eye for detail and mood helps me transform simple shots into vibrant visual content. Alongside my creative side, I have a deep interest in Malaysian politics, reflecting my curiosity about society and current events.

This combination of visual storytelling, culinary expression, and political awareness gives me a unique perspective and the ability to create content that is both engaging and thoughtful.
